Dorsky Museum App

This was a collaborative project with Nina Guido and Abby Carola. Our collective task was to create an app that would make people more excited to go to the campus art museum, The Dorsky. Together we researched, created wireframes, designed the UI and created a working prototype in Figma. We designed this app to be accessible and enhance the museum experience of any user, regardless of age or artistic knowledge.
