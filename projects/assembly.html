Assembly

Assembly is a concept for a restuarant in New Paltz, NY. It features a simple brunch menu and a thrift store attached to the restaurant. The brand was designed to feel contemporary while having universal appeal to all demographics. This is a complete style guide of all print and digital materials for the brand.
