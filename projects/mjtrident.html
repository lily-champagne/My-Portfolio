MJ TRIDENT Branding

This is branding for TRIDENT, a software built in-house at MJ Engineering and Land Surveying P.C. in Clifton Park, NY. The company wanted the TRIDENT logo to merge their design language with a modern, techy style. Once the branding was approved, I designed the website using a HTML/CSS site generator. The design of the website expands on the modern style of the logo, while also incorporating the updated company branding, I helped to develop.
