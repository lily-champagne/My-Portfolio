Dotty Lou's BBQ

Dotty Lou’s BBQ sought to establish its brand identity, including logo design, brand colors and fonts, and a style guide. The goal was to create a brand identity that was cool, classic, and clean, resonating with their audience. Collaboration and feedback guided the creative process, resulting in a successful brand identity package that positioned Dotty Lou’s as a standout player in the barbecue industry.
